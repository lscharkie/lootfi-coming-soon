Position Sizing Lab

Same signals, different sizing. How you size positions matters as much as which coins you pick. We test 5 sizing methods across 5 strategies to find what works best for each signal type. All tests use 30% vol target and 7bps fees.

Equal Weight
Every position gets the same capital. Simple, no assumptions about conviction.
Inverse Vol
Lower-vol coins get more capital. Risk parity — equalises risk contribution per position.
Momentum Weighted
Stronger signals get bigger positions. Concentrates capital in highest-conviction trades.
Risk-Adjusted
Weights by signal strength divided by volatility. Balances conviction with risk.
Kelly
Weights by expected return / variance. Mathematically optimal sizing for compounding.
